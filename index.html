About Me

Hello, my name is Daniel,

I just moved to Denmark 4 years ago from Indonesian where I lived for 10 years. My mother is Indonesian and my father is Danish. I moved to Denmark to continue my education and hopefully work in the tech industry, develop my programming skills and to be able to continue working in the tech industry for many years to come.

Below I talk a bit about the different companies I have worked for in the past as my CV is only an overview of my previous experiences with the different companies and this is a more detailed version of it.

At WhyWait I worked at a small start-up company where I primarily worked with the front end part of the website using html, css, javascript and a css framework called Bootstrap. WhyWait was a start-up company like Linkedin where they created a platform to connect candidates for recruitment and vice versa.

Dwarf is a large digital agency which is located in Frederiksberg, where I also worked as a front end developer where I used html, css together with a css framework called Bootstrap. I worked with them once a week while I was training.

Pinploy is a startup company where I worked as a full stack developer, where I used AngularJS for the frontend and Express, Typescript and MySQL for the backend part and made sure the code had no issues so it was ready to be implemented.

At Smartpage I worked on different websites for various clients, where I used Razor template engine and C# on the front end and used MySQL by retrieving data and displaying it on the page with a framework called .Net to handle and keep track of all the files which you work with by using a folder structure which is known as MVC (Model View Controller).

Just after my termination from Smartpage during the coronavirus period, I began working at Mimer. During my time at Mimer I was one of the developers in charge of the development of the website where I used Nuxt.js which is a free and open source web application framework based on Vue.js, Node.js, Webpack and Babel as the primary go to technology. Along with Nuxt.js I used Vuetify which is a CSS framework based on Material Design where I was able to use the various styling components that is offered on the Vuetify web site. I Integrated Trustpilot, Mailchimp, Typeform as well as created a rating system and fixed small minor functionality issues that the site had on a day to day basis

I have just gotten into Copenhagen Business Academy where I will be starting on 31st of August studying Computer Science. It is a 2.5 year degree and finishes in January 2023.
